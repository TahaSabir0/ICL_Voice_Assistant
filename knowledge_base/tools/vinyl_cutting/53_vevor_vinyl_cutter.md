## Documentation

### From: SOP-Vinyl

Standard operating procedures: Vinyl Cutter

Process
Computer controlled machine used to cut out vector-based designs from sheets of vinyl

Equipment
28” USCutter TITAN 3

Personal
Protective
Equipment
• Make sure loose-fitting items and long hair is properly secured

Required
Training
• Review and Observe ICL safety practices and general guidelines
• Lab supervisor led training specific to vinyl cutter process
• Review manufacturers operating manual

Inspection
Before use
• Make sure mechanical parts are un-obstructed
• Inspect blade and cutting head before use

hazards
• Blade failure due to wear or incorrect installation
• Puncture wound from changing blade
• Loose-fitting items or hair getting caught in the machine
Safe
operating
procedures
• Keep hands and loose-fitting items or long hair away from the cutting area
• Make sure machine is unplugged before performing any mechanical repair
